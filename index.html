MyHavtorn

MyHavtorn is a small company from Sweden's Bollebygd Valley that has sustainably grown and harvested its own sea buckthorn for the past 100 years. To avoid irritating preservatives, MyHavtorn freshly makes their sea buckthorn oil just for us. Their vitamin- and EFA-rich products work wonders on sensitive skin & rosacea.
